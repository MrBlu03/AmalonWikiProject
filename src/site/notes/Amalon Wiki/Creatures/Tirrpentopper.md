## Description
A magically attuned creature native to [[Amalon Wiki/The Kingdoms/High Valomor\|High Valomor]], known for its unique ability to accelerate to incredible speeds, effectively turning itself into a living projectile. The Tirrpentopper has a distinctive streamlined form that aids in its rapid movement.

## Habitat
- Natural environment: [[Amalon Wiki/The Kingdoms/High Valomor\|High Valomor]] territories, particularly around areas rich in magical energy
- Geographic location: Most commonly found near magical institutions and centers of [[Magic and Technology/Magic Systems/Astral Magic\|Astral Magic]]
- Territory size: Relatively small, but highly mobile within their chosen area

## Biology
- Size: Similar to a small deer
- Lifespan: Unknown due to their elusive nature
- Diet: Unknown, theorized to feed on ambient magical energy
- Reproduction: Rarely observed
- Special abilities: Can accelerate to bullet-like speeds, using their body as a projectile for both hunting and defense

## Behavior
- Social structure: Primarily solitary
- Activity patterns: Most active during periods of high magical activity
- Hunting/Feeding habits: Uses its acceleration ability to strike prey or gather resources
- Defensive mechanisms: Primary defense is its ability to accelerate rapidly to escape threats

## Interaction with Civilizations
- Domestication status: Untamed; attempts at domestication have proven unsuccessful
- Cultural significance: Studied by [[Amalon Wiki/The Kingdoms/High Valomor\|High Valomor]] scholars for their unique magical properties
- Economic value: Limited; primarily of academic interest
- Threats to humans: Generally non-aggressive but dangerous if startled due to their acceleration ability

## Conservation
- Population status: Unknown, difficult to track due to their speed and elusiveness
- Threats: Habitat disruption from magical experimentation
- Protection measures: Protected within [[Amalon Wiki/The Kingdoms/High Valomor\|High Valomor]] magical preserves

## Additional Information
- Common names: Living Bullet, Magic Dasher
- Related species: None known
- Historical significance: Subject of study in [[Amalon Wiki/The Kingdoms/High Valomor\|High Valomor]] for understanding natural manifestations of magic
- Notable incidents: Multiple recorded instances of Tirrpentoppers accidentally colliding with magical barriers due to their speed
